iTech Team's
Inauguration

The iTech Team is a group ran by Jacob & Charles at Washington Academy. We cherish online learning and the technology world. The iTech Team engages on a weekly basis in group activities. In these meetings we do various things, in the past we have fully disassembled an Alienware M15x and Macbook Pro. We have designed the meetings to helps kids and adults to become more comfortable. There are many people that are afraid to use a computer or any sort of technology and we would like to change that. Many of the online tools out there make your life easier in so many ways.

Our website is designed so people can go home from a meeting and recap by themselves what was covered. We have numerous tutorial videos and more to come on how to use many of the leading education tools out there today. These videos take you through step by step on how to cary out the objective.
